We are a young adult ministry of Parkside church for those between the ages of 18-35. Through praise, the preaching of God's Word and Christian fellowship we seek to glorify Christ and grow in our relationship with Him. Join us on Wedneday nights at the Loft located at 31200 Solon Rd.
