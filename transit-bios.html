Meet Deanna Wolford

Deanna grew up in Aurora, Ohio and has one brother, she grew up going to church but didn’t have much faith. She attended college in Tennessee where she took an interest in gardening and did a double major in Natural Resources (that is, Forestry & Geology) and French Literature. She was diagnosed with bipolar 1 disorder shortly after graduating from college and moved back to Aurora. She began working at a grocery store, and then an engineering company and has lived in Cleveland from 2015 to 2017 in a Catholic Worker house as a volunteer, she had mental health episode and moved back to Aurora, where she's lived since 2017. She currently works at a grocery store in Solon. Her dad passed away from cancer in June 2022 she began attending church again when he was dying because she needed to figure out what she believed. The church she attended was far away, so she started looking for churches closer to Aurora. She usually has to work on Sundays, so she searched for churches with Sunday evening services and found Parkside. She also remembered that the church she attended said that Parkside was a “good church”. She found Transit on the Parkside website and started attending in Summer 2023 where she looked forward to Transit each week. She started attending a church in Aurora a few months later. She accepted Jesus as her Savior, and is so happy now. In her free time she likes to run, knit, read books, and listen to music.
