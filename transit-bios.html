Meet Hayley Pausch

Hayley started attending Parkside in February of 2021, she very quickly fell in love with the solid preaching and made her way to Transit a few weeks later where she has planted her roots and hasn't missed a week. Meet Zeke Callaghan

Zeke is a 22 year old who resides in Broadview Heights. Jesus led him to Transit in June of 2023 where he was eager to grow in faith after a season of bewilderment. He encourages Christians to be surrounded by the truth of God's word and to have fellowship with other believers. He is grateful for Transit and everyone who has welcomed him in and encourages all to be a part of it whether starting out your faith journey, if you have been walking with the Lord for years or if you're unsure of what to believe about this world. Traveling is one of his big passions and he hopes to visit every continent in his lifetime. He loves working with computers, playing tennis and hanging out with his big poodle who is well behaved... sometimes.

Meet Hannah Lerner

Hannah is a 27 year old who grew up in Brazil as her parents were missionaries, she speaks Portugese and Spanish. Her favorite treat is cake and her and her husband Dane are huge Cavalier fans. Her first time at Transit was an overwhelming one showing up late and being intimidated by the amount of people there. After saying to her husband that they should just leave with the intention to slip out and never come back they were relieved to be greeted by Cameron Cronk who kindly greeted them and made them feel welcomed enough to continue showing up. They are both thankful they pushed past their nerves to continue coming, making tons of friends and getting an extra dose of the word from Matt Ross each week which would have been missed out on had she turned around on the first day.

Meet Logan Daw

Meet Mackenzie Ellman

Mackenzie came to the Parkside Green branch a few times in her undergraduate years. Upon graduating she desired to be involved in an evening group and figured she could find one at the main campus. She first attended Transit in the summer of 2021 but felt too young compared to others and picked up new work in the meantime. Two years later she eased herself back into Transit and became a regular attendee. She enjoys hanging out with friends and through that has found a love for board and card games, mini golf and hiking. She also enjoys biking around her neighborhood, kayaking, cleaning and learning how to cook and bake new recipes.

Meet Dane Lerner

Dane is Cleveland born and raised. He loves anything that has peanut butter in it. He enjoys cooking and eating together with others. After decades of prayer for his wife the two crossed paths a few years ago and are now (as of 5 months) happily married! A cool testimony from him is that when he was 12 years old he went to the Loft when it was still called “the warehouse” held for the middle schoolers. He constantly prayed for all the people that would be attending that building. The same blessed building that today Transit is now held. Where he continues to pray for it.

Meet Jemima Ukwela

Jemima has been involved in Transit since around 2020, after Standpoint (the college age ministry) became a part of Transit. In being a part of Transit these couple of years, it has been and continues to be a special space for her in her young adults years — through connecting with others weekly who desire to live for Christ and share Him with others, praying and encouraging one another as we face similar life circumstances, and enjoying community in fun happenings (i.e., game nights, volleyball, grabbing Mitchell's ice cream). If one is looking for a place to build meaningful connections in this way, she hopes you find the same experience of Transit as a place you feel invited to join and welcomed in!
